    About NERICT

    North Eastern Region Inter College Tournament (NERICT) is an inter-college tournament organised by the students of School of Technology, Assam Don Bosco Unversity, Azara Campus. It is an initiative taken by the students for the first time in the university. The main objective of organising this tournament is to discover and provide a platform to showcase the young talents of the students around the region as well as to build a relation of Unity among the students from various Universities and Institutes in the Entire North Eastern Region of India.

Unity Through Sports..! Promoting Youth's Talent..!

Date: 20th-21st Oct 2016

Inauguration: 9:00 am 20th Oct 2016

Venue: School of Technology, ADBU
